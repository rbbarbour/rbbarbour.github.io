About Me

My name is Rick Barbour and I have been a resident of Houston, TX for the past 10 years along with my wife and two young kids. I received a Bachelor's of Science in Mechanical Engineering from Worcester Polytechnic Institute in 2006 and have been working in the oil and gas industry ever since. I started out as a field engineer with Schlumberger in Youngsville, Louisiana but since then my career has taken me all over the world including countries like England, Norway, Malaysia, Russia and China!

I have always enjoyed making small programs in order to help make my job easier, and what initially started as a hobby has pivoted into my current role as a Senior Support Services Engineer with OneSubsea. I currently design and develop apps and BI reports using the Microsoft Power Platform. Since I started this role, I have been consistently looking to expand my programming knowledge which is why I have started taking the MERN Stack Certificate Course with MIT and created this digital portfolio as a result.
