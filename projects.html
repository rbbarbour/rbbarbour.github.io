Project Work

Below are some featured projects that I have worked on over the past few months as part of the MIT MERN Stack Certificate Course

PacMen Factory

This program generates a PacMan with random velocities in the X- and Y-directions each time the button is pushed. The PacMen will continue moving in this randomly generated direction until they hit a boundary at which point they "bounce" and move in the opposite direction. As the PacMen move, their mouths open and close by cycling through images based on their X-direction and status.

Completed on 28-Jul-2022

Real Time Bus Tracker

This program utilizes near real-time data from the Houston METRO Authority to track a random bus that is currently running within the Houston Metroplex. The bus number and route number are presented in the top left of the map and also by clicking on the marker. A different random bus can be tracked by pushing the button again and the map center will adjust accordingly to locate the new bus.

The ODATA feed from Houston METRO updates irregularly (roughly every 15 seconds), so the tracker is set to retrieve data every 5 seconds and updates accordingly.

Completed on 11-Aug-2022
